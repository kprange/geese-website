Mount Laurel Township, NJ orders the gassing of 133 Canadian Geese.

GooseWatch Wisconsin, GWW, advocates for compassion for, tolerance of and co-existence with urban wildlife, and promotes humane approaches to resolving conflicts with our wild neighbors.

 

Canada Geese

 

It is a known fact that removing Canada geese via roundups/gassing doesn't deter other geese from coming back to the same site the next year, yet this unethical practice continues with no solution across the county. Year after year here in Wisconsin alone, communities like Delafield, Twin Lakes and Oconomowoc Lake, known for their large bodies of water as tourist attractions, corral flightless adult and fledging Canada geese into the backs of vans where they gas and kill them. There is a better way.

 

Our long term  goal is to change the law in Wisconsin to protect Canada geese from round ups/gassing and provide alternative safe options to solve the issue. Gassing Canada geese is not the solution.
